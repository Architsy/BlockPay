How to Use This Website

Welcome to the Solana Blockchain Suite! Here's how to get started:

  1. Create a Wallet: Click on "Create Wallet" to generate a new Solana wallet. Save your private key securely.
  2. Load a Wallet: Use "Load Wallet" to access an existing wallet using your private key.
  3. Check Balance: View your wallet's balance in SOL.
  4. Send Transactions: Transfer SOL to another wallet by entering the receiver's address and amount.
  5. QR Code Features: Generate QR codes for your wallet or scan QR codes to send transactions quickly.
  6. Transaction History: View your past transactions.
  7. Latest Block Info: Check the latest block details on the Solana blockchain.
  8. Upload to Google Drive: Upload files to Google Drive (requires Google credentials).
  9. Validate Keypair: Verify if a public and private key pair is valid.
